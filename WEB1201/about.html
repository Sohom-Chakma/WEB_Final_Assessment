About Us

BEAN & CO is a vintage-style cafe that aims to serve our customers the best coffee, beverages, and desserts. We serve our signature fresh-brewed coffee, a variety of teas, and other hot and cold drinks. Besides beverages, you can also enjoy our freshly baked pastries, desserts, and cakes prepared daily. We select our ingredients carefully, using only the freshest ingredients to maintain the highest quality standards and ensure every product we serve meets our high expectations.

Our cafe provides you with a cozy and comfortable atmosphere designed with vintage style in every detail, from the furniture to the overall decor. Our cafe is a perfect place for you to gather with friends, have a break, enjoy your teatime, and capture memorable photos. Our cafe is located near several workplaces and office buildings, which is the ideal place for busy professionals to grab a re-energizing coffee before work, enjoy a relaxing break during the day, or treat themselves to a well-earned treat.

Our mission is to serve the highest quality of coffee daily, helping our customers feel energized and prepared for the whole day. We aim to let the customers feel satisfied with our every product and always think of our cafe when they are craving for coffee and desserts. Looking ahead, we are also excited to introduce more new and special menus and expand our reach by opening more stores in different places to let people around the world have a try at our products and feel impressed by it.
